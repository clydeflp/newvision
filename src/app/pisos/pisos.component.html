Wood: Description and Uses

Wood flooring is an elegant and warm option for interiors, widely used in homes and commercial spaces. It is made from different types of wood, such as oak, walnut, or pine, each with unique tones and grain patterns. There are also alternatives like laminate or vinyl flooring that mimic the appearance of natural wood..

This type of flooring is durable and creates a cozy atmosphere. However, it requires proper maintenance to protect it from moisture and wear. Its versatility and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for kitchens, living rooms, and bedrooms.

Ceramic Tiles: Description and Uses

Ceramic tiles are a popular flooring option for bathrooms, kitchens, and other interior spaces due to their durability and resistance to moisture. Available in a wide variety of colors, textures, and designs, they allow for the creation of modern and elegant environments.

This type of flooring is easy to clean, does not absorb liquids, and withstands daily use. Additionally, its water resistance makes it an excellent choice for areas with high exposure to moisture, such as bathrooms and kitchens.

Decorative Tiles: Description and Uses

Decorative tiles are a stylish and functional flooring option, often used in kitchens, bathrooms, and laundry rooms. They come in a variety of patterns, colors, and materials, such as ceramic, porcelain, or cement, allowing for creative and unique designs.

These tiles are durable, easy to clean, and resistant to moisture, making them an excellent choice for high-traffic and wet areas. Their versatility and aesthetic appeal make them popular for both modern and traditional interior spaces.
